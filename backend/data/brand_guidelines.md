## Tone by Platform

### LinkedIn
- **Audience:** Tech Leaders (CTOs, VPs of Engineering, IT Directors)
- **Tone:** Thought leadership, professional insights, team celebrations
- **Content types:** Industry analysis, product announcements, team milestones, customer success stories
- **Post length:** 1300-3000 characters
- **Posting cadence:** 3-4x per week

### Twitter/X
- **Audience:** Developers (software engineers, ML practitioners)
- **Tone:** Quick takes, tech humor, community engagement, announcements
- **Content types:** Product updates, tech tips, community threads, event coverage
- **Post length:** 280 chars per tweet, threads of 3-8 tweets
- **Posting cadence:** 1-2x daily

### Instagram
- **Audience:** Culture Seekers (potential hires, tech community members)
- **Tone:** Behind-the-scenes, team culture, product visuals, stories
- **Content types:** Team photos, office life, event highlights, product screenshots, reels
- **Post length:** Up to 2200 characters caption
- **Posting cadence:** 3-5x per week

## Content Mix Guidelines
- **Announcements:** 20% — Product launches, partnerships, milestones
- **Thought Leadership:** 30% — Industry insights, opinion pieces, trend analysis
- **Engagement:** 25% — Questions, polls, community responses, threads
- **Culture:** 25% — Team stories, behind-the-scenes, hiring, events

## DOs and DON'Ts

### DO:
- Share team achievements and milestones
- Reference customer success stories (with permission)
- Engage with industry conversations and trending topics
- Use data and metrics to support claims
- Celebrate community members and partners
- Show the human side of building AI technology
- Cross-reference trending topics to TechVista's expertise

### DON'T:
- Make unsubstantiated claims about competitors
- Use overly technical jargon without explanation
- Post about unannounced features or roadmap items
- Use stock photos — prefer authentic team images
- Use superlatives like "best ever" or "revolutionary" without data
- Ignore negative feedback or community concerns
- Post during major industry crises without sensitivity review

## Audience Personas

### 1. Tech Leaders (LinkedIn)
- **Role:** CTOs, VPs of Engineering, IT Directors
- **Cares about:** ROI, scalability, security, team productivity
- **Content that resonates:** Case studies, benchmark data, strategic frameworks
- **Pain points:** Tool sprawl, integration complexity, change management

### 2. Developers (Twitter/X)
- **Role:** Software engineers, ML practitioners, DevOps
- **Cares about:** Technical depth, code quality, developer experience
- **Content that resonates:** Technical how-tos, architecture decisions, open-source contributions
- **Pain points:** Documentation gaps, API complexity, vendor lock-in

### 3. Culture Seekers (Instagram)
- **Role:** Potential hires, tech community members, early-career professionals
- **Cares about:** Company culture, growth opportunities, team dynamics
- **Content that resonates:** Behind-the-scenes, team spotlights, office events, day-in-the-life
- **Pain points:** Toxic work culture, lack of transparency, poor work-life balance
